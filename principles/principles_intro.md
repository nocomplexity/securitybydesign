## Introduction

Security principles are the foundation of any robust security architecture. They provide timeless, reusable guidance that helps architects and engineers make consistent, defensible decisions — regardless of which technologies or products are used.

In this section, you will learn why principles matter more than specific solutions, and how applying them early prevents costly redesigns later.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this section, you will be able to:

- Explain what a security principle is and how it differs from a requirement or control

- Understand why principles must be applied at every process step within cybersecurity activities

- Recognise and apply all key Security by Design principles
